Brownies!

Sugary deserts that keeps u awake all night.

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate.
  • 100g unsalted butter, very soft.
  • 250g caster sugar.
  • 4 large free range eggs, beaten to mix.
  • 1tsp vanilla essence.
  • 60g plain flour.
  • 60g cocoa powder.
  • 4 large free range eggs, beaten to mix.
  • Baking or brownie tin and rack.

Procedure

  1. Heat oven to 180C, break up the chocolate and melt it over simmering water. Put it to the side to cool after melted.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar with a mixer until fluffy. Then beat in the eggs, and vanilla essence.
  3. Mix the chocolate with the mixture and sift the flour and cocoa powder, then gently stir in.
  4. Spread mixture evenly onto the tin.
  5. Bake in oven for 20 minutes until firm but still fudgy. Leave it to cook outside of the oven for several min.
  6. Lay the brownies on a rack and allow it to cool off before cutting into pieces.

How to make the BEST Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich ever!

This is a wonderful snack to have whenever your sorta hungry. This fun snack can be used for many purposes. Some include throwing, showing off your sweet sandwich making skills and of course, EATING!
Note: People with Peanut Butter allergies should avoid eating this snack

    Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
    Instructions
  1. Grab ingredients and a knife
  2. Take out two slices of bread
  3. Spread Peanut Butter evenly onto one slice
  4. Spread Jelly onto other slice
  5. Combine both pieces of bread
  6. Enjoy

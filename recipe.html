Peanut Butter Sandwich

How to make a peanut butter Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • A knife

Instructions:

  1. Get two pieces of bread
  2. Get out the peanut butter
  3. Get out a knife for the peanut butter
  4. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  5. Stick the pieces of bread together and enjoy!

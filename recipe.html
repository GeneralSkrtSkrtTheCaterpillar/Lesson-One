Cheesecake

Funfacts about Cheesecake

  • Cheescake is not really cake, it is a baked cheese custart pie with a crust
  • July 30th is National Cheesecake Day
  • The largest cheesecake ever made weighed 4,703 pounds. Philidelphia Kraft Foods made the cake on January 25, 2009 in Mexico

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups of graham craker crumbs
  • 3 Tbsp.sugar
  • 1/3 cup of butter,melted
  • 4 packages of Philadelphia Cream Cheese,softened
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp.vanilla
  • 4 eggs

How to Make it

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Combine the graham cracker crumbs with the sugar and butter and mix
  3. Press the mixture down on a 9 inch deep pan
  4. Beat the cream cheese with 1 cup of sugar and vinilla with mixer untill blended
  5. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed, making sure the egg is all blended
  6. Pour mixture over crust and bake for 55 minutes
  7. Finally refrigerate cheesecake for 4 hours and enjoy!
